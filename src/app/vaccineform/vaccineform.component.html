Vaccine Safety and Monitoring

Vaccines undergo rigorous testing and monitoring to ensure their safety and efficacy. Vaccine safety is continuously monitored through:

  • Pre-licensure clinical trials
  • Post-licensure surveillance systems
  • Adverse event reporting systems
  • Vaccine safety research

Addressing Vaccine Misconceptions

It's important to address common misconceptions about vaccines to make informed decisions. Some common misconceptions include:

  • Vaccines cause autism (scientifically disproven)
  • Natural immunity is superior to vaccine-induced immunity
  • Too many vaccines overwhelm the immune system
  • Vaccines are not necessary for diseases with low prevalence
