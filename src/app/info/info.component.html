Understanding Vaccine Ingredients

Vaccines contain a combination of ingredients that help stimulate the immune system and provide immunity against diseases. Common vaccine ingredients include:

  • Inactivated or weakened viruses or bacteria
  • Adjuvants to enhance the immune response
  • Preservatives to prevent contamination
  • Stabilizers to maintain vaccine potency
  • Other substances for vaccine production and administration
