Precautions

Ensuring the safety and well-being of your little one is our top priority. Before proceeding with any vaccinations, it's important to keep a few precautions in mind. Always consult with a pediatrician or healthcare professional who can guide you through the vaccination process and address any concerns you may have. Ensure that your child is in good health before receiving a vaccine, as certain illnesses or conditions may require adjustments to the vaccination schedule.
